In table \ref{tab:flu_test} we see the results of 10 random trials.  For each trial you are given the total number of participants, the number of participants who tested positive for flu, the number that actually developed the flu (true positives), and the percentage of correct diagnoses.  Use this table and your knowledge of \emph{binomial distributions} to answer the following questions.
